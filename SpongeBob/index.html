The Legendary Tale of Bentley & SpongeBob

Bentley once went to boating school with SpongeBob. They both failed the driving test, but Bentley failed even harder (he drove into the ocean floor cafeteria by accident).

Later, Bentley and SpongeBob went to college together. Bentley majored in "Underwater Basket Weaving," while SpongeBob majored in "Bubble Studies." Their dorm smelled like seaweed and jellyfish jelly.

One time Bentley wrote an essay so bad that even Plankton didn’t want to steal it. But SpongeBob said, “Wow Bentley, this is the best thing I ever read!”
